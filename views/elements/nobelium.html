Nobelium

Nobelium does not occur naturally in the Earth’s crust. It was first synthesized in 1966 by Russian scientists from the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research (JINR) in Dubna, Russia under Georgi Flerov. Earlier claims to have synthesized “nobelium” beginning in 1957 were shown to be erroneous.
